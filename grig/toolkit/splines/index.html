Splines (grig.toolkit.splines)¶

Introduction¶

The grig.toolkit.splines module contains classes and functions to represent and evaluate splines. Algorithms are based on the Fortran routines DIERCKX by P. Dierckx reimplemented to N-dimensions, and accelerated to C-like speeds through use of the numba package.
